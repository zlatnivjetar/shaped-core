Most common questions

Can I change or cancel my booking?

Yes, there is a “Manage Booking” link in your confimation email, where you can either reschedule or cancel your booking. If you prefer, contact us directly.

Is parking available?

Yes, free private parking is provided for all guests

What kind of breakfast is served at Preelook?

Breakfast options include: Continental, Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten-Free and Buffet

How close is the nearest beach?

The nearest beach is only 50m from the apartments.

How far is Preelook from center of Opatija?

Preelook is 2.5km from center of Opatija, which is a 5min drive.
